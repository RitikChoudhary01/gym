FAQs

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What are your gym timings? +
We're open Monday to Saturday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Sunday from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Our trainers are available across all slots to guide you.
Is the 3-day free trial truly free? +
100% free. No card required. No hidden conditions. Just book your slot, walk in, and experience the gym for 3 full days including access to all equipment and one complimentary trainer consultation.
Do you provide diet plans? +
Yes! Every Pro Warrior and Elite Champion member gets a customized diet plan created by our certified nutritionist, tailored to your body type, goal, and food preferences.
Is the gym safe and suitable for beginners? +
Absolutely. We have a dedicated beginner induction program where you'll learn correct form, gym etiquette, and safe lifting techniques before jumping into full training.
Do you have facilities for women? +
Yes! We have a fully equipped Women's Zone with separate changing rooms, lockers, and dedicated female trainers.
Can I pause or cancel my membership? +
Yes. You can pause your membership for up to 30 days per year at no extra cost. Cancellations are accepted with 15 days notice. No lock-in on monthly plans.
